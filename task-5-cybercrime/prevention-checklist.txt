PART B:- PREVENTION CHECKLIST

Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

General Safety Tips
• Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts; avoid using the same password everywhere. 
• Enable two-factor authentication on important apps like email, banking, and social media. 
• Do not click on suspicious links or download files from unknown sources. 
• Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into banking or personal accounts on unsecured networks. 
• Regularly update your apps and operating system to protect against security vulnerabilities. 
• Think before sharing personal information (phone number, address, college details) online.

Financial/ UPI Safety Tips 
* Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money—UPI PIN is only required to send money. 
* Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
* Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or untrusted sources.
* Do not share OTPs, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.

Reporting CyberCrime 
* Report any cybercrime immediately at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
*  Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud assistance.
