PART B:- Social Media DO’s and DONT’s 

Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

 Do’s: 
• Maintain a respectful and professional tone in your posts and comments. 
• Think before posting—consider how it may affect your reputation.
• Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal   information.
 • Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
 • Use social media for learning, networking, and building positive connections.
 
Don’ts: 
• Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
• Avoid sharing sensitive personal or academic information publicly. 
• Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying. 
• Avoid oversharing your location or daily activities. 
• Do not plagiarize content or share others’ work without credit.
